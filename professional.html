Cover Letter

To whom it may concern,

Hello, my name is Cole Thorpen and I am searching for entry-level data science positions primarily, but not exclusively, in the Seattle area. Throughout my professional and academic careers, I have gained much experience that's proven valuable in many fields. My technical career began during my undergraduate tenure at Gonzaga University where I studied computer science. During this time, I focused on developing my skills in mobile and web-based application development. I am the best candidate for any data science/analysis role because of my determination and willingness to learn. Not only do I wish to perform complex analysis, but I want to fully understand what I am working with. My curiosity fuels my work and pushes me to become a better version of myself every day. I am specifically passionate about the field of sports and health/fitness. My goal is to use my analysis skills to accurately predict how susceptible athletes are to injuries, and find patterns in data that allow us to see why injuries may occur. However, due to my general curiosity for data, I want to learn about many different industries.
